Comments and questions

  • The 'Inbox' view is where you get the boxes with items you want to store in warehouse.
  • Select some items from inbox and move to some shelf
  • The 'Warehouse' view shows the current state and statistics of the warehouse
  • You can drag-drop boxes from one shelf to another.
  • Only yhe top box from each shelf can be moved at once.
  • You can delete boxes by dragging them and dropping to the delete icon.
